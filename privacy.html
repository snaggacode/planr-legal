PLANR.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Planr is operated by Pinnacle Apps (ABN: 25 403 393 572), based in Perth, Western Australia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Planr app.

By using Planr, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and account type (Solo Athlete, Client, or Coach) when you register.
  • Profile data: Optional information including your date of birth, height, weight, fitness goal, activity level, and sex — used to calculate personalised nutrition targets and program recommendations.
  • Training data: Session logs, exercise names, sets, reps, weight, and RPE values you enter during training sessions.
  • Nutrition data: Food items, calorie and macro logs, and nutrition targets you set or receive from your coach.
  • Body composition data: Bodyweight entries and physique photos you choose to upload.
  • Coach-client data: If you are a coach, information about your clients including their training and progress data. If you are a client, your data is accessible to your assigned coach.
  • Usage data: AI feature usage counts (for rate limiting purposes).
  • Messages: Messages sent between coaches and clients within the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Planr app and its features
  • To calculate personalised nutrition targets and TDEE estimates
  • To generate AI-powered training programs, session suggestions, and meal recommendations via the Anthropic Claude API
  • To enable coach-client communication and program delivery
  • To process subscription payments via RevenueCat and Apple/Google payment systems
  • To send transactional emails including password reset emails via Resend
  • To improve and maintain the app

AI Features

Planr uses the Anthropic Claude API to power AI features including program generation, session suggestions, meal suggestions, and body composition analysis. When you use these features, relevant data (such as exercise lists, nutrition logs, or physique photos) is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. This data is used only to generate your requested output and is not used to train AI models. Please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy for more information.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database service with servers in the Asia Pacific region. Physique photos are stored in Supabase Storage with private access — only you and your assigned coach (if applicable) can access them.

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Your coach: If you are a client, your training data, nutrition logs, progress data, and messages are accessible to your assigned coach.
  • Service providers: We use Supabase (database), Anthropic (AI features), RevenueCat (subscriptions), Apple/Google (payments), and Resend (email) to operate the app. These providers have their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

Physique Photos

Photos you upload for body composition tracking are stored privately and are only accessible to you and your assigned coach (if applicable). We do not use your photos for any purpose other than displaying them within the app and generating AI body composition analysis when you explicitly request it.

Subscriptions and Payments

Planr offers paid subscriptions (Planr Pro and Planr Coach). All payment processing is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store your payment card details. Subscription management is handled via RevenueCat.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we permanently delete all your personal data including training logs, nutrition data, body metrics, photos, and messages. This process is irreversible.

Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Law and other applicable regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at velastudio.app@gmail.com or use the "Delete my account" option within the app.

Children's Privacy

Planr is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
